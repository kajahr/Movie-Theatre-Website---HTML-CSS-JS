Home Movies with Kajah and Astra

A San Francisco couple, discovering cinema together.

Phenomena (1985) Dario Argento

Astra
This was a weird psycadelic horror fantasy. It was Jennifer Connelly's brekout role!

Kajah
This ended up being my favorite Argento movie. It always satifying seeing what could have been a standard, fun supernatural film expressed through the strange and at times incomprehensible mind of Argento. Also was interesting that a young JC got to work with Argento before she moved on to costars and collaborators like Henson and Bowie.

No Time to Die (2021) Cari Joji Fukanaga

Astra
A long-winded action film.

Kajah
The first hour or so is amazballs. One of the most inspired Bond beginnings. Starts to slow to a crawl when the attempt to explain the pretty strightforward story in a complex way to set Bond up a difficult situation. Was looking forward to Malek but he's not given much time to grow or show off his villan chops. I enjoyed the way it all wrapped up but damn....it's almost three hours y'all.
